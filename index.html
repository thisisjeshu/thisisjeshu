Welcome to Shalowm Covenant Church

Bringing Peace, Love, and Faith to Your Life

Greetings in the name of the Lord!

We are delighted to welcome you to Shalowm Covenant Church, a place where faith, fellowship, and community converge. Our church is dedicated to spreading the message of peace, love, and salvation through Jesus Christ.

About Pastor Shashi

Pastor Shashi is a devoted servant of the Lord, leading our congregation with wisdom, compassion, and a deep understanding of God's Word. With years of experience in ministry, Pastor Shashi is committed to guiding individuals and families on their spiritual journeys.

Our Vision

“The Kingdom of God…on earth as it is in heaven.”

Our Mission

To introduce people to an authentic relationship with God through an encounter with Jesus the Messiah.

To experience community through relationships of honour.

To equip God’s people for their ministry through healing and training in the power of the Holy Spirit.

To engage God’s people in doing the works of Jesus in His Body and the community.

To impact the community through expanding the Kingdom of God.

Our Core Values

Supernatural Transformation: God desires divine health and healing of the whole person.

God’s Word: We open ourselves to hear and obey His voice through prayer and study of the Bible.

Relationship with God: Developing an intimate relationship with Him.

People are Valuable: Every person is created in God’s image.

Identity: We are given a new identity as Saints of the Most High.

Excellence: God is worthy of our very best.

Teamwork: Every believer is a minister, with gifts to be discovered and deployed.

Growth: We are co-labourers with God in expanding His kingdom.

No Condemnation Culture: God’s purpose is restoration and freedom, not condemnation.

What We Believe

We believe we are a significant part of what God is doing in India and all over the world. We honor and bless every congregation that names Yeshua (Jesus) the Messiah as Lord.

We worship One God revealed in Three persons.

Yeshua is Lord and Messiah.

Sin separates us from God.

Yeshua the Messiah is the only solution to sin.

The Holy Spirit gives us the power to live in victory over sin now and into eternity.

God calls us to deliver His message and do His works.

We believe the teachings of the Bible and strive to put them into practice in everyday life.
